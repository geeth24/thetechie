The Techie

Geeth Gunnampalli (aka. The Techie), from Coppell, Texas, loves to code and produce music. He was inspired to take up a new hobby of producing music in 2018 by one of his friends Zaen Yunus (aka. ZNO). He started his production in EDM but failed to produce well for a year. He never consulted Zaen for help because he didn't know him well back then. They were almost complete strangers. Then he joined TWO14 SOUND (A record label made by Rith Bhattacharya), and that's when he got an opportunity to work with Zaen and many other producers to up his game. Zaen and the other producers gave him feedback and helped him identify the loopholes in his music. After 2 months, he then realized that people like to listen to Rap & RnB songs with vocals rather than EDM beats with no vocals. So, he made a transition to Rap & RnB. Zaen helped him a lot through the transition. He taught him how to produce. 6 months after joining TWO14 SOUND he can produce 100x better than he was able to before.
